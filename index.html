Methodology & Data Sources

How this calculator generates estimates

üìä Data Sources

Benchmarks are compiled from WordStream (16,400+ campaigns), LocaliQ, Triple Whale (7,000+ accounts), Databox, and other industry reports covering 2024-2025 data. Google Ads data represents Search campaigns unless otherwise noted.

üßÆ Calculation Method

Estimated clicks = Budget √∑ Industry CPC. Conversions = Clicks √ó Industry Conversion Rate. Revenue = Conversions √ó Your AOV. ROAS = Revenue √∑ Budget. Budget allocation follows the 40-50% search, 20-30% social, 10-15% display framework.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Limitations

Industry averages mask significant variance. Your results depend on ad quality, landing pages, targeting, competition, seasonality, and business factors. This tool provides directional estimates only‚Äînot predictions.

üéØ Best Practices

Start with 70% of budget on proven channels, 20% on growth experiments, 10% on testing. Track your own benchmarks over time. Optimize for ROAS, not just CPC. Consider customer lifetime value, not just first purchase.
